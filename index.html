    About Us

    Our Company History and Culture.

    Since its humble establishment in 2004, Sf Furniture Sdn Bhd. has been progressing steadily towards being both a promising furniture manufacturer and exporter on par with meeting up today's market demands. Located in one of the furniture pioneer lands of Johor, Bukit Bakri Muar, our production factory has a land area spanning over 80,000 sq feet with its main build up area of 40,000 sq feet. The blend between cutting-edge technology, workmanship and best-graded materials is what we commit in earning our customers' guaranteed.

    Being a furniture wholesaler and manufacturer, all of our products undergo distinct QC(Quality Control) from craftmanship, design, packaging to shipping for ensuring only the excellent goods will be delivered to our customers as quality is our main emphasize in Living World. Our team of Research and Development staffs has since put in meticulous attention to maintain premium quality materials being used in every stage of the production.
